## Proximity to Failure

**Refalo et al. 2023 — Influence of Resistance Training Proximity-to-Failure (Sports Medicine)**
- Graded dose-response with proximity to failure; 0-3 RIR threshold for effective stimulus
- Used in: [muscle-mapping.md](muscle-mapping.md) (RPE scaling curve)
- https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9935748/

**Refalo et al. 2024 — Dose-Response Meta-Regressions (Sports Medicine)**
- Nonlinear relationship: drop-off from RPE 10→8 small, RPE 8→6 large
- Used in: [muscle-mapping.md](muscle-mapping.md)
- https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s40279-024-02069-2

## EMG and Muscle Activation

**PMC — Bench Press EMG: Five Bench Inclinations**

- Used in: [muscle-mapping.md](muscle-mapping.md) (bench contributions)

## Sex Differences

**Frontiers in Physiology 2018 — Female Fatigue Resistance**

- Used in: [sex-differences.md](sex-differences.md)

**Stronger By Science — Strength Training for Women**
- Women can handle more relative training volume
- Used in: [sex-differences.md](sex-differences.md)
